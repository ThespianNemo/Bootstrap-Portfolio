About Me

Having always been an inquisitive person, my professional interests naturally led me into the world of IT. My IT career evolved over the years, from programmer to sr. systems analyst, IT entry-level training instructor to IT training curriculum manager.

As my career progressed, I found my interests moving more toward the creative, social side of things and began pursuing knowledge in the field of full stack web development. The internet is the first place almost everyone goes to when looking for a product or service. If you don’t have a website that lures customers, you’re losing business.

Having a varied background in retail IT systems, educational publishing and development, my skills lend themselves to individuals/companies wanting to create a distinctive web presence.
